Development of a new
offering: Leading financial
services institution

Creating a new approach to financing & purchasing a property

A leading international financial service company asked itself whether it should create a major real estate financing portal to support its prime product: housing loans. We reframed this question to “let‘s conduct research to see if people are interested in (immediate) online financing,” & built a Minimum Viable Product to validate this question. The MVP was actually included in a property portal, thereby enabling us to validate quantitatively whether this strategy made sense before making an investment of Euros in the eight-digit range. The final MVP proofed to be so successful that the organization decided to invest! Ever since, we have accompanied the division in building out its new offering, which has achieved stellar ratings in all app stores.

Project Scope: Business Model Validation, Customer Validation, Concept Development, MVP, App Development

Creating a new type of telco that customers love

A large multi-country telecommunication carrier asked two essential questions: “why do customers hate telco carriers in general?” &, in case it was able to answer this question, “how can we consequently disrupt the European telco market?”

The concept has already been validated by key regulatory authorities.
