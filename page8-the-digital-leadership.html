The Digital Leadership
Program

The Digital Leadership Program is an immersive training program for companies wanting to optimize & accelerate their digital transformation.

The core target group is senior management & executives.
The training will allow you to understand, manage & implement the components of your Digital Transformation.

Trainings are held by LEADing Practice –the #1 provider of Enterprise & Industry Standards globally – &
Global University Alliance – the world’s leading academic research network focused on business.

Main content blocks &
objectives

Understanding the findings & implications of the Outperformer Study – the world‘s largest enterprise innovation & transformation study

Understanding the key components of Enterprise Innovation & Transformation across Strategy, Execution & Governance

Translating these concepts into an Enterprise Strategy & Portfolio Approach for your own enterprise

Developing a specific approach to your enterprise & its objectives, as well as implementation & change-management

Building a world class digital innovation & transformation team

The format.

The Digital Leadership Program is highly adaptive & tailored to the needs & objective of your enterprise.

  • Targeted to an executive audience
  • To the point – presenting the key points precisely & concisely
  • End2end – from strategy to execution
  • Based on the findings & implications of the
    Outperformer Study, the largest research study ever conducted in the area of Enterprise Innovation & Transformation

Location & Duration

The Digital Leadership Program is held at the Château du Grand Perray – the home of LEADingPractice. The location is absolutely stunning, & up to 18 people can be hosted on the premises of the castle.

The training is typically held as an offsite, as to ensure focus. Other locations are conceivable however, including your own place of business.

The program normally lasts for 3-7 days& includes top-level events & team building activities.
